# ASSCAT Campus AI

## Problem
Old student portal has too many confusing menus. Students waste time clicking 10+ buttons just to check clearance or schedules.

## Industry 5.0
AI helps students, not replaces them. The app listens to natural speech instead of forcing users to learn complex menus. Human staff focus on important tasks while AI handles simple questions.

## User Stories

1. As a student, I want to say "check my clearance", so that I don't need to click through 5 menu pages.

2. As a busy student, I want to ask "what's my schedule today?", so that I quickly know my classes.

3. As a student with bad internet, I want the app to work offline, so that I can still access my data.
